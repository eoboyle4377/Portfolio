About Us

Our Purpose

Dish Diaries was created to provide a platform for home chefs of all levels of experience to share their favorite recipes with others. Food brings us together and though the cuisine may change in different parts of the world, it is one thing that we all have in common. Those that visit our site are free to browse family recipes and individual creations shared by those that hold them close to their heart. Post your own recipes and hear from others who love them as much as you do.

How Do I Share a Recipe?

Visit our contact page and fill out a brief form to put your recipe out there for all to see. Post an image, description, ingredients and cooking instructions. That should be all one needs to put your meal onto their table so the whole family can enjoy. Check on your post at a later date to view feedback on your dish, including ratings and comments from people who tried out your meal.
