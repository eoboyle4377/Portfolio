About Us.

Millions of dogs, cats and other domesticated animals are stray, lost or abandoned on a regular basis. We at House of Snouts are lucky enough to take in a few of these wonderful animals and find them the happy home they deserve. We opened our doors in 2019 and have been serving the Cleveland area ever since. We pride ourselves in giving our animals the love and affection they need, but nothing compares to the joy of finding them a permanent home. Our team of full-time employees and volunteers love to match candidates with their perfect companion, so if you are looking to fill a pet-sized void in your life, or simply have more love to give, then please fill out our quick contact form. Taking the first step in pet adoption brings us one step closer to saving the lives of these precious animals.
