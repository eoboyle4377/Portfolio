About Us

Hoagies Heroes is dedicated to providing the most innovative and delicious hogie creations we can, while also giving back to the community. We load our sandwiches with flavorful ingredients most have never thought to put together with the intention of creating an experience you can't find anywhere else. You'll be so pleasantly surprised with our hand-crafted combos you'll wish you weren't so full after one! We work closely with our communities to donate portions of our profits to organizations we see making a difference. Our mission is to feed the hungry and use the proceeds to making the world a better place, one neighborhood at a time.
