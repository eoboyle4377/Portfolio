The Many Faces of Venom

Venom is in fact a shapeless, alien symbiote that must take the form of a host in order to thrive. Because of the parasitic nature of Venom, he has inhabited the bodies of many different characters over the years. Below are just a few of the more popular adaptations of Venom found in comic books and movies.


Eddie Brock

Venom's best known host

Peter Parker

Spiderman himself was the first to be bonded to Venom

Mac Gargan

Otherwise known as Scorpion, another villianous rival of spiderman.

Flash Thompson

Infected and became Agent Venom, a superhero adaptation.

Malekith

Captured and tortured Venom, eventually turning himself into a super weapon.

Lee Price

Acquired Venom through the black market to become a Super Villian

Tel Kar

One of Venom's early hosts, used shapeshifting to go undercover during war.

Ann Weying

Former wife of Eddie Brock, fused with Venom to save her life after she was shot.
