Omega Brewing Company (Est. 2022)

Omega Brewing is a rapidly growing brewery and taproom located just outside of Cincinnati, Ohio that opened its doors in August of 2022. We focus on developing high quality craft beers from locally sourced ingredients to meet the needs of our consumers. The company was founded by current owner, Alphonso Omega, whose passion for beer began at the age of 16, when he mistakenly took a warm Pabst Blue Ribbon for his can of soda. Over the years he has perfected the art of brewing and his knack for business has helped turn a small basement brewery into a 5,000 square ft. house of hops.

The company now prides itself in using locally sourced ingredients and serving the Cincinnati community. Rotating seasonal menus mean that with each changing of the season, come new ideas and new ingredients. The quality of our beer and our service is what defines Omega Brewing Company.
