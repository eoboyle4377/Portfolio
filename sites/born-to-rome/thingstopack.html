What to bring will usually depend on the season and location. You will probably want to create a checklist of your own, but we have prepared a short list of essentials for your European getaway. Here are certain items you will want to pack with you on your trip to reduce the stress and make the most of your time on your adventureâ€¦

What To Bring:

What Do I Need? Why Do I Need It?
Passport Certain documents will be required for international travel.
Proper Attire Depending on the season, you will want to dress accordingly. With many fine dining options, dress clothes are a must!
Travel Wallet Keep your valuables close to vest. Your currency and personal items will stay safe and organized in a secure travel wallet.
Power Adapter If you live outside of Europe, you will need an outlet adapter for your cameras, cellphones and other rechargeable batteries.
Travel Insurance Local insurance will not cover costs due to an accident, so invest in a travel policy for peace-of-mind.
Just-in-case Bag Do not put all of your eggs in one basket. If your luggage is lost, make sure you have emergency items on hand.
